GeoBRAIN

Learning to reasoning over Geoscience knowledge from the pespective of AI. You can now find a suitable foundation model in GeoBRAIN most suits you and create your own project.

Find More

GeoBRAIN is a collection of AI models and data resouces for Geoscience. Large language models (LLMs) have achieved great success in general domains of natural language processing. and our K2 shows the potential in AGI for Geoscience. We establish GeoBRAIN for serving geoscientist with advanced AI technique. What's more, we provide up-to-date data sources like GAKG and research assistant SciAide (Coming soom) to help geoscience to do research more efficiently. And we also preserve a community for the interaction between geoscience and computer science.

We are actively developing GeoBRAIN, and welcome contributions from both geoscience and computer science community. Follow GeoBRAIN to keep up to date.

Geoscience Knowledge

Get started by open-source Geoscience knowledge. Thanks to GAKG, it will be automatically updated and published the latest geoscience academic knowledge graph.

Open-source AGI

We will develop several Geoscience large language model, e.g. K2. geoscientist can find a suitable foundation model that most suits the project of geoscience.

Geoscience Community

We are going to maintain a community that can not only benefit geoscientist but also can help us develop advanced AI technique with the feedback from the geoscience groups.
